Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Let cool before serving.

Nutritional Information

Nutrient Amount per serving
Calories 250
Protein 5g
Fat 10g
Carbohydrates 35g

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Fry the pancetta with the garlic.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with the cheeses.
  4. Combine spaghetti with pancetta and remove from heat.
  5. Quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture.
  6. Serve with extra cheese and pepper.

Nutritional Information

Nutrient Amount per serving
Calories 400
Protein 15g
Fat 20g
Carbohydrates 45g

Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and cook the chicken until browned.
  2. Add onions, garlic, and ginger and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add curry powder and cook for another minute.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  5. Cook until chicken is fully cooked and sauce thickens.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve with rice.

Nutritional Information

Nutrient Amount per serving
Calories 350
Protein 25g
Fat 20g
Carbohydrates 15g
